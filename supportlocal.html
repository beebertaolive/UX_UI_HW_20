School Group Project

By Berta Oliveira, Brian Harris and Sarah Barrett
Ideated and developed by the group

Problem

People are looking for ways to shop locally & small. Small business owners want to be noticed as real people that are part of their community.

Project Proposal

COVID-19 continues to change the way people live and shop. Amid racial and political conflict, many people want to support minority-owned businesses or businesses that share common beliefs. People understand the need to “vote with their dollars” to support businesses in their communities.
